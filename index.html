About me

I'm a full-stack software engineer with strong expertise in backend architecture, payment integrations, and mobile development. Over the past years, I've built and maintained complex systems across Android, web, and API platforms, supporting thousands of users and high-volume business operations.

I specialize in scalable Laravel backends, secure financial workflows, and performance-driven dashboards powered by caching and automated background tasks. I've integrated multiple payment processors including Stripe, Moncash, NATCASH, and Square, designing reliable and efficient transaction flows for FinTech and business applications.

On the mobile side, I have extensive experience developing Android apps with advanced offline capabilities, local data caching, and optimized database queries.

I enjoy solving challenging technical problems, whether it's building intelligent engines for predictions and analytics, optimizing business logic, or automating printing systems.

I'm passionate about clean architecture, performance, and creating products that are both impactful and user-focused.
